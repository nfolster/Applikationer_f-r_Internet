Welcome to Tastyrecipes.com!


On this website we have unique and extrodinary recipes for your enjoyment. But what is a recipe? A recipe is a set of instructions that
describes how to prepare or make something, especially a culinary dish. It is also used in medicine or in information technology (user acceptance).
A doctor will usually begin a prescription with recipe, usually abbreviated to Rx or an equivalent symbol.
